Mingjing Du

Associate Professor

School of Computer Science and Technology

Jiangsu Normal University

Mingjing Du is an Associate Professor with the School of Computer Science and Technology at Jiangsu Normal University. He received his Ph.D degree in computer science from China University of Mining and Technology, China. His research interests include data mining and machine learning, specialized in cluster analysis and time series analysis.
